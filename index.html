Quick Information

About C-RAAT

C-RAAT (pronounced "see-raat") is the Centre for Research and Applications of AI Technologies, housed within the Faculty of Computing at Mohammad Ali Jinnah University, Karachi. Founded in July 2025, the centre serves as a hub for AI research, education, and community engagement.

The centre takes its name and spirit from the Arabic word صراط — "the path" — symbolising purposeful direction toward responsible and impactful innovation in AI. C-RAAT works at the intersection of global AI developments and Pakistan's national priorities, with a particular focus on regional languages, low-resource NLP, and applied machine learning.
