About C-RAAT

Centre for Research and Applications of AI Technologies

Founded in July 2025 at MAJU's Faculty of Computing — a centre dedicated to AI research, capacity building, and public understanding of artificial intelligence.

Identity & Name

C-RAAT — صراط

C-RAAT stands for the Centre for Research and Applications of AI Technologies. It is pronounced "see-raat", echoing the Arabic word صراط (ṣirāṭ) — meaning "the path" or "the way."

This name is intentional: it positions the centre as a guide along the path of AI innovation — purposeful, directed, and responsible. Just as صراط connotes a clear and righteous way forward, C-RAAT aims to channel AI research and education toward meaningful impact.

The centre was founded in July 2025 within the Faculty of Computing at Mohammad Ali Jinnah University, Karachi, with a mandate spanning original research, public outreach, and community skill-building.

What We Do

Our Mission

C-RAAT's mission spans three interconnected pillars — each essential to building a sustainable, impactful AI ecosystem in Pakistan.

  • Conduct Research & Development in AI Address global trends, national priorities, and MAJU's internal operational challenges through applied and foundational AI research. Contribute to the global body of knowledge while remaining grounded in Pakistan's context.
  • Advance Public Understanding of AI Demystify artificial intelligence through training programmes, public seminars, lectures, and community outreach. Make AI knowledge accessible to students, professionals, policymakers, and the general public.
  • Build Capacity by Nurturing AI Skills Equip students, faculty, and the broader community with practical AI skills through workshops, summer schools, and collaborative learning opportunities. Prepare the next generation to engage with AI purposefully and responsibly.
